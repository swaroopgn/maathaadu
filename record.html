ನಿಮ್ಮ ಧ್ವನಿ — record the words in your own voice

The games normally use a computer voice. Record yourself (or ajji!) saying these, and the games will play your voice instead — research says hearing amma or appa say a phrase makes kids far more likely to use it with you.

Tap rec, say the word warmly and clearly, tap stop. It saves on this device only. me plays your version, × removes it. Rows turn green once your voice is saved.
